# Top Tips to Reduce Your Smartphone Use

Do you use your smartphone too much? A lot of us spend hours every day using our phones.

Maybe you don't worry about how much you use your phone. Maybe you need to use it for work.

But maybe you want to try to use your phone less.

It isn't easy to reduce your phone use. But there are lots of ways to do it.

Some people like to put their phone in a different room for part of the day.

Joshua Becker from the website Becoming Minimalist says it's a good idea not to charge your phone next to your bed. That's so you won't use it late at night or early in the morning.

There are also apps to help you reduce the time you spend on your smartphone.

But some apps aren't so good for us. Courtney Lindwall from a website called Consumer Reports says we should mute app notifications. Notifications can be distracting, and make us use our phones more.

Some people lock their phones in boxes. Others buy phones that don't have cameras, the internet or apps, so they won't want to use them as much.

It can also be a good idea to spend time on hobbies, sports or talking to friends — things that can help us put our phones down.

If you want to use your phone less, there's no one answer. We all have to find the way that works best for us!
