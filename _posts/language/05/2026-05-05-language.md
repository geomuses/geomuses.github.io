# How to Plan the Perfect Day Trip

It's always nice to go on a long holiday. But it's not something we always have the time — or money — to do.

That's when it's time for a day trip!

Day trips can be nice, because they don't come with all the stress of planning for a long holiday.

Travel blogger Samantha Brown says that, when planning a day trip, you should choose one big thing you really want to see or do in a place and plan around it.

If you have several things you want to see, travel blogger Sara Scott says you may need to choose the ones that will take the least time!

When you have chosen what to see, Brown says you should then look for a place nearby where you can get some food — maybe a restaurant or café with good reviews on Google Maps.

If you want to save money, you could also just take a picnic or a box lunch!

After that, plan how you'll travel from place to place. Brown says it's best to start with a destination that isn't more than two hours from your home — because you still have to go back home at the end of the day, and you don't want to be tired just from traveling.

Also, check if you need to buy tickets for attractions early — you don't want to get there and find out you can't get into your one big thing!
