# How to Avoid Stress When You Have a Deadline

A lot of people feel stressed when they have a deadline that's getting closer.

Feeling stressed about a deadline is normal, but it's not very good for your health. So what can you do?

The health website Calm says you should look at how much time you have before the deadline, and make a plan. Writing a plan on paper can make the deadline feel less overwhelming.

You should also find the most important parts of the work and plan to do those things first.

But it's still important to take short breaks — if you don't, you will only feel more stress, and your work also won't be as good.

The Calm website says it's also good to talk to other people. Maybe they can help you think of ways to do the work — and talking to them can also just help you feel less overwhelmed!

But Jess Baker, a business psychologist, told the _BBC_ that sometimes you may just have to ask for the deadline to be changed. Maybe you think the deadline is too soon, or you need more time to do a good job.

It isn't always possible to change a deadline, but you can still ask.

People sometimes say that it's better to finish something late than not to finish it at all!
