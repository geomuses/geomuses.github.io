# Clothes Top List of Online Returns — Survey

You can buy almost anything online these days, but shopping online can sometimes feel like a bit of a gamble.

If you're buying clothes, for example, a T-shirt might look great online. But when it arrives, it might be too big, or you might not like the fabric.

Luckily, many stores let people return things they bought online. And according to a survey, clothes are the most common online purchases that people return.

The survey, done by a company called Overnight Glasses, asked 1,800 shoppers about items they bought online in 2025 and later returned.

Clothing made up about 38% of online returns. T-shirts and tops were among the most common returned items of clothing, with a 72% return rate.

When asked why they returned certain items, many said the size was wrong, or the clothes just didn't look good when they wore them.

Footwear made up 14% of online returns. However, it actually had a higher return rate than clothing — more than half of all shoes purchased online were returned.

Electronics made up about 12% of all returns. Many people said they had returned a phone case because it wasn't the right size. Screen protectors and chargers were also regularly returned.

Other items in the list included car parts and home goods.

The survey also found that people buy certain items but don't plan to keep them. This includes party decorations, which are returned the next day 65% of the time!
